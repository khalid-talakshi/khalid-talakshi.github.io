F1 is all about speed, strategy, and skill. You need to be fast, need to be smart, and need to be consistent. However, there are ways to influence your chances of winning before the lights go out. Some comes in the form of pre-race strategy planning, some in the form of upgrades to the car. However, one of the most important ways to do this is by getting the best spot on the grid before the race even starts. Qualifying is a critical part of the F1 weekend, and in some races is the most important part of the weekend.
